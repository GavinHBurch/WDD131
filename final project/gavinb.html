Custodial Training Design - About Gavin Burch

Gavin Burch works for Black Ridge Elementary as a Head Custodian and has been doing this for two years now. I also worked as a Night Lead for Orem High School and a head sweeper at Belmont Elementary. I have learned what to do as a head custodian from trainings and papers I have been provided in the past two years. I hope this will help all new custodians or old to know what to do as a head custodian.

Gavin has been taught in small parts of HVAC, Plumbing, and Electrical. I have not recieved any certifications on these things but can help with basic maintence of these departments.

Prior to working as a Head Custodian at Black Ridge, I worked at Orem High School as a Night Lead. A Night Lead is there to help the Head Custodian during the nights with any problems that may araise. I had to help with Electrical, Plumbing, and HVAC on a daily basis to help maintain the school.

Gavin is currently going to BYU Idaho for a computer programming degree and is hoping to go to trade school after to learn Electrical or Plumbing.
